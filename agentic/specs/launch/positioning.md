# Positioning

## One sentence
**HomePilot is the assistant that behaves like a Chief of Staff for your life and your work.**

## Differentiation
* Chatbots answer.
* HomePilot *runs a workflow*: gather → decide → brief.

## Safety posture
* Default is read-only + advisory.
* Capabilities are enabled via **tool bundles** (Context Forge virtual servers).
* Action tools (email, tickets, etc.) are opt-in addons with confirmation UX.
