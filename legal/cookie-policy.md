# Cookie / Local Storage Notice (Draft)

## 1. What may be used
- Essential session/auth storage
- Preference storage
- Optional analytics storage (only with consent where required)

## 2. Consent
- Non-essential tracking stays off until consent.
- Users can withdraw consent and reset preferences.

## 3. Retention
- Keep storage duration minimal and documented by category.

## 4. Open-source note
Self-host operators must disclose their own analytics/tracking configuration.
