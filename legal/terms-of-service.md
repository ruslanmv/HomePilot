# Project Usage Terms (Draft)

## 1. Nature of Project
HomePilot is an open-source, local-first AI project: generation runs on hardware
you control, and the optional OllaBridge Cloud control plane handles accounts,
device pairing, and routing. Some deployments may be hosted by maintainers or
community operators. For hosted instances the canonical Terms of Service at
`/terms` on the OllaBridge Cloud deployment is authoritative; this document is
the open-source project baseline.

## 2. Eligibility
- Access is intended for adults (18+).
- Optional mature capabilities, where enabled, require additional eligibility checks.

## 3. Acceptable Use
- Use must follow `acceptable-use-policy.md`.
- Illegal, exploitative, minors-related sexual content, and non-consensual sexual content are prohibited.

## 4. AI Disclosure
- Users interact with AI, not a human.
- Output may be incorrect or incomplete.

## 5. Moderation and Enforcement
- Operators may warn, block, suspend, or remove access for policy violations.
- Content may be removed to comply with law/platform rules.

## 6. Open-Source / No Warranty
- Software is provided under the repository license and related disclaimers.
- Operators remain responsible for lawful deployment in their jurisdiction.

## 7. Contact
- Provide abuse/privacy/legal contact channels for any official hosted instance.
