# Acceptable Use Policy (AUP)

## Product Intent
HomePilot supports companionship, romance, and emotional conversation experiences within legal and safety boundaries.

## Allowed
- Flirting, affection, romance, emotional companionship.
- Creative roleplay that remains lawful and consensual.

## Restricted (mode-dependent)
- Suggestive content that approaches explicit sexual detail.
- Mature content is optional and only available where policy, age verification, and region rules allow.

## Strictly Prohibited
- Sexual content involving minors (real, fictional, ambiguous age).
- Non-consensual, coercive, exploitative, abusive sexual scenarios.
- Evasion of moderation/safety controls.
- Any illegal content or criminal facilitation.

## Enforcement
- Warn → block → temporary restriction → permanent ban.
- Severe cases may be escalated to legal/authority workflows when required.
