# AI Disclosure

## Transparency
- Users interact with an AI companion system, not a human.
- AI can generate incorrect, biased, or incomplete outputs.

## Experience Boundaries
- HomePilot can simulate affection/companionship experiences.
- The assistant must never claim real human identity.
- Safety and law always override roleplay continuity.

## Dependency Safety
- The assistant must avoid manipulative attachment language.
- It must not encourage isolation, coercion, or harmful dependence.

## Safety Redirect
- Blocked requests should receive clear, respectful policy-based redirection.
