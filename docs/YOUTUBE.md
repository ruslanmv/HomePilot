# Make a YouTube Video from Your Writing 🎬

Turn any article or essay into a **full YouTube video**, a **vertical Short**, and
a **square teaser** — with narration and captions — right inside HomePilot.
No editing skills needed. This guide is written for everyone, not just techies.

---

## What you get

From one piece of writing, HomePilot can create:

- 📺 A **full-length video** (16:9) for YouTube
- 📱 A **Short** (9:16) for YouTube Shorts, TikTok, and Reels
- ⬛ A **square teaser** (1:1) for social posts

Each one comes with **spoken narration** and **on-screen captions** — the big,
bold, word-by-word style you see from top creators.

---

## How to make one — step by step

1. **Open Creator Studio** in HomePilot and start a **new Video project**.
2. In the **Details** step, choose **Script Source → "Essay / Script."**
3. **Paste your article** (or paste the link to it). HomePilot uses *your own
   words* — it doesn't rewrite them.
4. Pick the format you want (YouTube Video, Short, or Teaser) and press
   **Generate**. HomePilot turns your writing into scenes with visuals.
5. Review the scenes. You can tweak any of them, then let it create the video.

That's it. HomePilot writes the captions, adds the voice, and builds the video.

---

## Turning on captions and picking the look

When you export the video (the **Export** step), you'll see a few simple
switches:

- ☑️ **Burn subtitles into the video** — keeps captions always on screen.
- ☑️ **Word-by-word captions** — the lively, one-word-at-a-time style that
  highlights each word as it's spoken (the "Shorts" look). Turn this on for
  Shorts and TikTok.
- **Background fill for stills** — choose how pictures fill the screen:
  - **Letterbox** — may show black bars (plain).
  - **Cover** — fills the screen, trims the edges a little.
  - **Blur fill** — fills the screen with a soft blurred background, **no black
    bars** (the most polished, recommended for Shorts).

Pick **Word-by-word captions** + **Blur fill** for the most professional result.

---

## A few friendly tips

- ✅ **Already have narration audio?** If your essay has an audio version, paste
  its link too — HomePilot will reuse that voice so everything sounds the same.
- ✅ **Captions look tiny?** Make sure "Burn subtitles" is on — HomePilot
  automatically makes them large and readable, bigger for Shorts.
- ✅ **Want it faster to make?** The full-length video reuses your existing
  narration; only the Short needs a fresh, shorter voice-over.

## Frequently asked

**Do I need a fancy computer?**
A recent gaming graphics card (like an RTX 4080 laptop) makes the visuals fast.
Captions, narration, and the video assembly work on modest machines too. See
[ESSAY_VIDEO_BUNDLES.md](./ESSAY_VIDEO_BUNDLES.md) if you want the recommended
"one-click" model packs.

**Will it change my words?**
No. Your writing becomes the narration exactly as you wrote it.

**Where do my files go?**
Everything is created on your own computer. Nothing is uploaded unless you
choose to.
