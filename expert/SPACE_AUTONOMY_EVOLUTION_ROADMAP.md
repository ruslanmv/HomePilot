# Space Autonomy & Self-Evolution Roadmap (Long-Term)

> Strategic R&D roadmap. Not a near-term production feature set.

---

## Alignment note

This roadmap extends the same Expert principles used today:

- local-first control planes
- policy-bounded autonomy
- deterministic fallback and governance
- auditable memory/provenance

It is intentionally staged and safety-constrained.

---

## Autonomy maturity ladder

### Level 0 — Assisted intelligence (current baseline)
- Human-initiated tasks
- no self-modification
- tool-assisted reasoning

### Level 1 — Mission autonomy
- bounded plan execution
- strict constraints and audit traces

### Level 2 — Adaptive autonomy
- policy-bounded adaptation
- simulation validation before deployment

### Level 3 — Controlled self-improvement
- signed update pipeline
- mandatory eval/safety gates
- rollback and quarantine mechanisms

### Level 4 — Distributed exploration intelligence
- multi-agent mission collaboration
- shared provenance graph + governance controls

---

## Safety non-negotiables

1. Human command authority always supersedes autonomous decisions.
2. No live self-modification in mission-critical systems.
3. High-risk action classes require explicit policy approval.
